Early Detection of Skin Disease

Skin treatment has become a necessity in countries like India where most of the working is in field work, where there is direct contact of skin with external factors.

Traditionally, the skin disease diagnosis is done by a dermatologist where they examine the patient based on visual inspection and perform several tests to come to a conclusion.It gets tricky when two or more diseases look alike, so you need a highly experienced dermatologist. The diagnosis process is tough because it's hard work, takes a long time, needs a deep understanding, and can have errors because it depends on personal judgment and humans. We have used Machine Learning based Approach called Convolution Neural Network (CNN) to help with this. It can look at lots of pictures of skin problems, find small details, and predict what the problem might be very accurately and consistently.
